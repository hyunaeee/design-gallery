A LIFEFORM OF LIGHT

Something swims the nebula with the light swallowed whole.
Having no name for it, we call it the cosmos.

SCROLL ↓
SEQUENCE 01

It breathes

Even in vacuum it expands and contracts. No heart, yet a rhythm. No lungs, yet a breath.

SEQUENCE 02

It scatters

The border of its body comes undone. What was one becomes thousands, bleeding into the dark.

SEQUENCE 03

It fills the room

There is no telling where it ends and the cosmos begins. The whole screen becomes one living thing.

SEQUENCE 04

You are one of them

From the same dust, returning to the same light. Somewhere among these particles, there is you.
